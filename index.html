Research Interests

Serotonin Neuroprotection

We study serotonin signaling in the retina to determine how it confers protective effects against glaucoma progression

Retinal Vasculature

We investigate how retinal ganglion cell function relates to local blood supply to uncover the role of the vasculature in glaucoma

Early Detection

We develop new tools to monitor glaucoma before it presents as vision loss to better identify patients in need of treatment

Single Cell Genomics

We study the genetic drivers for resilient cell types and apply these patterns to vulurable cell types to prevent cell death
